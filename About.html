Meet the Team

Angel Rinos

Angel Rinos is the dynamic leader of the team and the main coder at Spandex Tokuverse. With a profound passion for Tokusatsu that began in 2020, Angel's favorites include iconic heroes like Kamen Rider Decade, Gokaiger, Ultraman Zero, Ultraman Gaia, and Kamen Rider Blade. When not coding or expanding the Tokuverse, Angel can be found practicing Jiu Jitsu, where they bring the same dedication and intensity. Angel's technical skills are extensive, proficient in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, PHP, and Wordpress, which they expertly utilize to enhance our digital platforms. Under Angel’s leadership, our project continually evolves, pushing the boundaries of Tokusatsu appreciation and interactive engagement. Their unique combination of coding prowess and love for Tokusatsu makes Angel an invaluable leader at Spandex Tokuverse.

Aaron Montallana

Aaron Montallana is one of co-lead artists for the website, a reliable person who understands the assignment. Aaron is responsible for the website’s banner image. Additionally, he always gives 110% on the project. Aaron makes sure to consider all possible options before deciding on one that’s not only ideal but also considers its future effects. When not busy, he spends his time alone, improving his digital drawing skills; he always strives to grow into a better version of himself. He is adept at multiple mediums (oil or acrylic paint on canvas, charcoal, and traditional) for art pieces. Finally, he discovered his passion for Tokusatsu in January 2023; his favored Tokusatsu heroes are Kamen Rider Gotchard, Kamen Rider Wizard, Kamen Rider Double, Ultraman Blazar, and Ultraman Arc.

Minh Ngyuen

Minh Nguyen is the other co-lead artist for the blog. They designed the logo on the website’s header image. A creative individual who can create exactly what the team needs; they’re adept in digital art and sketching designs. Their skill with digital art also extends to 3D modeling. Additionally, they can storyboard excellently and are part of a team for a start-up animation studio. In their spare time, they enjoy playing card games with their friends. They started getting into Tokusatsu since September 2019, and their favorite Tokusatsu heroes are Ultraman Blazar, Kamen Rider Ex-Aid, Kamen Rider Leo, and Kamen Rider W.

Luan Du

Luan Du is a member of Spandex Tokuverse, his role is news reporter and translator of the team. His hobbies include watching tokusatsu shows, and Japanese dramas. Next, his skills is to update Tokusatsu news to let people know what the recent status is. He can translate and speak Japanese a little bit. His top 5 favorite tokusatsu heroes are Ultraman Tiga, Ultraman Z, Ultraman mebius, Kamen rider Faiz, and Kamen rider Ryuki. Finally, The show that made he started to watch tokusatsu is Ultraman Tiga.

Linh

Linh is another member of the team who acts as the secondary coder for the website. Hobbies include playing video games, collecting and building model kits, watching tv, and some drawing. Skills include proficiency in Python, C++, and Assembly coding. His top 5 toku heroes are Kamen Rider Snipe, DekaRed, Kamen Rider Vulcan, Kamen Rider W, and GokaiRed.
