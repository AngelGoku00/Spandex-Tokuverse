Ultraman Charity for the Young

By Luan Du
Published on April 25, 2024

There was an suddenly earthquake in the Oku Noto Peninsula, Wajima City. Many house and school in around by those got destroyed by the earthquake. As Tsuruno Takeshi (ultraman dyna) and Yoshioka Takeshi(ultraman gaia) see this news, they volunteer go to that area. They volunteer pass out of the food to the students and people around that area. After passing out the food, tsuruno and takeshi were singing together with people to cheer them up despite the area have hard time situation. And yet at the end many people were pleased and happy to see takeshi tsuruno (ultraman dyna) and yoshioka takeshi (ultraman gaia) to come their area for volunteering.
