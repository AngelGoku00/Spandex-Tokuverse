TokuTech

Welcome to TokuTech! We are a Tokusatsu community online team dedicated to spread Tokusatsu content to known fans and anyone interested. Our goal is to grow our community and for the world of Tokusatsu to be known to the world. We have everything: a character customization, news about con events, toys, leaks, and upcoming movie content, a wiki, fan art gallery, and so much more!
