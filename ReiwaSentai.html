Reiwa Super Sentai

Mashin Sentai Kiramager (2020–2021)

Mashin Sentai Kiramager aired from 2020 to 2021, marking the forty-fourth installment in the Super Sentai series. This season introduced a team known as the Kiramagers, who utilize the power of mystical crystals and their vehicle partners, the Mashins, to battle the evil Yodonheim empire. Kiramager's themes of brilliance, gems, and creativity set it apart within the franchise. The series is known for its imaginative storytelling and vibrant visual effects, highlighting the unique abilities of each Kiramager. Mashin Sentai Kiramager's characters are defined by their personal growth and the development of their artistic talents, which enhance their powers. Each member's resilience and creativity are crucial to the team's success. Kiramager's impact on the Super Sentai genre is noted for its focus on the arts and individual expression, resonating with a broad audience. The show combines action, humor, and drama, making it a memorable entry in the series. Mashin Sentai Kiramager is celebrated for its engaging battles, compelling characters, and a strong message about the power of imagination and emotion.

Kikai Sentai Zenkaiger (2021–2022)

Kikai Sentai Zenkaiger aired from 2021 to 2022, marking the forty-fifth anniversary of the Super Sentai series. This season featured a unique team that includes both human and robotic Rangers, fighting against the evil Kikaitopia Dynasty Tojitendo. Zenkaiger's theme of celebrating all past Super Sentai series through its "world of machines" concept distinguished it within the franchise. The series is notable for its innovative approach, integrating characters and elements from the entire history of Super Sentai. Kikai Sentai Zenkaiger's storyline revolves around the adventure and unity among diverse types of heroes. Each member brings a mix of nostalgic homage and new dynamics to the team. Zenkaiger's impact on the Super Sentai genre is significant for its meta-narrative that bridges generations of Sentai lore, appealing to both new and long-time fans. The show emphasizes the importance of legacy and teamwork, creating a vibrant and engaging viewing experience. Kikai Sentai Zenkaiger remains celebrated for its inventive storytelling, dynamic battles, and the heartfelt celebration of its heritage.

Avataro Sentai Donbrother (2022–2023)

"Avataro Sentai Donbrothers" is a standout series in the Super Sentai franchise, known for its unique blend of traditional Japanese folklore and modern settings. The series introduces a team of five members: Don Momotaro, the leader, along with Oni Sister, Saru Brother, Kiji Brother, and Inu Brother. Each member derives their powers through distinctive means, and they come together to battle against the Noto and the monstrous beings known as Hitotsuki. Don Momotaro leads with courage and strategic insight, while Oni Sister brings strength and fierce determination. Saru Brother offers agility and cunning, Kiji Brother provides wisdom and aerial skills, and Inu Brother contributes loyalty and tracking abilities. This dynamic team structure allows for complex character development and backstories that unfold throughout the series, enriching the narrative. "Donbrothers" maintains a balance of action, drama, and humor, adhering to the beloved Super Sentai formula while introducing new storytelling techniques. The series also features crossover elements, enhancing its universe with appearances from characters of previous series. Visually, "Donbrothers" is vibrant, with elaborate costumes and innovative special effects that heighten the viewing experience. Overall, "Avataro Sentai Donbrothers" is celebrated for its fresh approach and engaging additions to the Super Sentai legacy, making it a memorable and innovative series.

Ohsama Sentai Kingohger (2023–2024)

"Ohsama Sentai King-Ohger" is the 47th entry in the popular Super Sentai series, known for its unique blend of action, adventure, and colorful characters. In this installment, the series adopts an insect motif, where each Ranger is inspired by a different insect, adding a fascinating biological twist to their superhero identities. The team consists of five main members, each representing powerful insect characteristics that influence their abilities and weapons. Set against a backdrop that combines modern Earth with elements of royal intrigue, the Rangers are tasked with protecting the planet from alien invaders. The series emphasizes themes of environmentalism and coexistence with nature, reflecting through the insect-inspired heroes and their connection to their natural powers. Visually, "King-Ohger" impresses with its elaborate costumes and special effects that vividly bring the insect world to life within the Super Sentai universe. The narrative delves into the responsibilities of kingship and leadership, as each member learns to lead in their capacity while fostering teamwork and unity. The villains in the series are creatively designed to contrast the natural, organic qualities of the heroes, often embodying themes of pollution and destruction. Throughout its episodes, "King-Ohger" explores deep moral questions about duty, sacrifice, and the impact of human activities on the environment. Overall, "Ohsama Sentai King-Ohger" stands out as an engaging and meaningful series that combines superhero action with significant environmental messages, continuing to innovate within the Super Sentai franchise.
